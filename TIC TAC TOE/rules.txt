RULES OF TIC TAC TOE
--------------------

1. The objective of Tic Tac Toe is to get four in a row. 
2. You play on a four by four game board. 
3. The first player is known as X and the second is O. 
4. Players alternate placing Xs and Os on the game board until either oppent has four in a row or all sixteen positions are filled. 
5. X always goes first, and in the event that no one has four in a row, the stalemate is called a draw game.
